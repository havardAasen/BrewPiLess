### Backup

The utilities to create a backup, and restore settings, includes System
Configuration, Gravity Device, Remote Logging, Pressure transducer, and MQTT.

Go to <http://brewpiless.local/backup.htm> "Retrieve from Controller",
and then "Save..".  The downloaded file, `bpl.settings.json`, will be saved
to your computer.

To Restore,
Select file to "Load from", enter "username/password", and then "Restore..".
